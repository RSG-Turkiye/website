The 5th RSG-Türkiye Student Symposium continued the annual tradition of fostering computational biology education and networking among Turkish students and researchers.

ISCB SC RSG-Türkiye is a student-led, non-profit organization affiliated with the International Society for Computational Biology Student Council (ISCB SC). Our annual symposiums have been bringing together undergraduate and graduate students since 2012.
