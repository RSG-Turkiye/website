The 7th RSG-Türkiye Student Symposium was the first edition adapted for a virtual format in response to the COVID-19 pandemic. Despite the challenges, the symposium successfully connected students and researchers across Türkiye.

ISCB SC RSG-Türkiye has become a paramount student initiative, organizing events that contribute to the developing computational biology and bioinformatics society in Türkiye. We organize student symposiums attracting undergraduate and graduate students since 2012.
