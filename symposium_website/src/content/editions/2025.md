The 12th RSG-Türkiye Student Symposium was held as a satellite event to HIBIT 2025 at Medipol University, Istanbul.

## Keynote Speakers

- **Özkan Özdemir** — Artificial Intelligence and FAIR Principles in Health
- **Ogün Adebali** — Evolutionary Analysis of Functional Specialization in Protein Families
- **Nurcan Tunçbağ** — Discovery of Hidden Biomolecular Connections in Modeling Complex Diseases
- **Vildan Enisoğlu Atalay** — A New Approach in Drug Design: Computational Chemistry

## About

ISCB SC RSG-Türkiye is a student-led, non-profit organization affiliated with the International Society for Computational Biology Student Council (ISCB SC). Our annual symposiums have been bringing together undergraduate and graduate students since 2012.
