The 8th RSG-Türkiye Student Symposium was held online due to the global pandemic, demonstrating our community's commitment to maintaining scientific exchange during challenging times.

ISCB SC RSG-Türkiye has become a paramount student initiative, organizing events that contribute to the developing computational biology and bioinformatics society in Türkiye. We organize student symposiums attracting undergraduate and graduate students since 2012. Being less formal and more student-focused, our symposiums provide a platform where students can actively participate, present their work, and connect with researchers.
