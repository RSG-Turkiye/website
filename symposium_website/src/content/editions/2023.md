The 10th RSG-Türkiye Student Symposium was held in 2023 at the METU Informatics Institute in Ankara, marking a decade of annual symposiums.

## Speakers

- **Dr. Ramazan Terzi** — Data Mining and Big Data in Bioinformatics
- **Prof. Dr. Tolga Çukur** — Computational Imaging and Machine Learning (Bilkent University)
- **Prof. Dr. Kaan Orhan** — Dental Informatics and AI (Ankara University)
- **Dr. Burçak Otlu** — Computational Biology (UC San Diego)
- **Dr. Ahmet Gökçen Er** — Bioinformatics Research

## About

ISCB SC RSG-Türkiye has become a paramount student initiative, organizing events that contribute to the developing computational biology and bioinformatics society in Türkiye. We organize student symposiums attracting undergraduate and graduate students since 2012.
