The 11th RSG-Türkiye Student Symposium was held in October 2024 at the Mehmet Akif Ersoy Art Center in Pendik, Istanbul.

## Highlights

- **Keynote I:** Prof. Dr. Gökhan Kaçar on molecular dynamics simulations of nanoscale drug carrier systems
- **Keynote II:** Dr. Mehmet Baysan on data science and clinical bioinformatics (COSAP, Turkey Genome Project)
- **Workshop:** Dr. Kübra Narcı on building reproducible bioinformatics pipelines with Nextflow and nf-core
- **Panel Discussion:** Clinical Bioinformatics Startups — From Idea to Market
- **Biostatistics Session:** Prof. Dr. Handan Ankaralı
- **Student Talks** (×3) and **Poster Session & Networking**
- **Career Session:** Round Table Meetings

## About

RSG-Türkiye is a student-led, non-profit organization affiliated with the International Society for Computational Biology Student Council (ISCB SC). Our annual symposium fosters interdisciplinary collaboration among undergraduate students and early-career researchers while promoting equal opportunities in education.
